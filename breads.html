Whole Wheat Bread

Our everyday whole wheat loaf, available in 50% or 80% whole wheat based on your preference.

  • Choose between 50% and 80% whole wheat
  • 50% is softer, 80% is denser and more robust
  • Made for everyday use
  • No additives or preservatives

Ingredients
Whole wheat flour, refined flour, yeast, salt, water.

What to Expect
50% gives a softer, lighter bite while 80% has a stronger wheat flavor and denser structure.

Customization
Available in 50% or 80% whole wheat. Choose based on your preference for softness vs whole grain intensity. As whole wheat content increases, the loaf becomes denser with a more pronounced wheaty flavor; lower whole wheat gives a more balanced taste and softer texture.

Baker's Details

Whole wheat flour: 50%
White flour: 50%
Hydration: 70-75%
Salt: 2%
Yeast: 1%
Lemon juice: 0.2%

Dough weight: ~870g
Final baked weight: ~800g

Weight
Approx 800g

Best consumed within 2-3 days.
Store in a cool place and avoid direct air.
