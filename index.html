Understanding Our Bread

Why is your bread priced higher than regular bread?

Our breads are made in small batches using time-intensive fermentation and without commercial additives. This requires more time, care, and ingredients compared to industrial bread, which is produced for scale and speed.

What are bread improvers?

Improvers are additives used in commercial baking to speed up production and standardize results. We do not use them, allowing the dough to develop naturally.

What is preferment?

Preferment is a technique where a portion of the dough is fermented in advance. In our process, this stage typically takes 12-15 hours. This allows the dough to develop naturally over time, improving flavor, structure, and overall quality without the need for additives.

Why our bread feels different

Without additives, our bread has a more natural texture and structure. Whole wheat breads are denser, reflecting the grain itself.

Why does our bread dry faster?

Our breads are made without bread softeners and preservatives. Because of this, they can dry out more quickly if left exposed to air or stored in the refrigerator. This is a natural characteristic of bread made with simple ingredients. See storage for details.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place your order via WhatsApp. We'll confirm availability and schedule your bake.

When will my order be ready?

Orders are typically ready the next day. A 24-hour notice is preferred as we bake in small batches.

Do you bake every day?

We bake in limited batches based on orders. This helps us maintain quality and freshness rather than producing large quantities in advance.

What is the shelf life of your bread?

Our breads do not contain preservatives. They are best consumed within 2-3 days. We suggest 2 days. For longer shelf life, you can freeze (not refrigerate) the slices and toast them when needed.

Are your breads free from gluten, dairy, or nuts?

No. Our breads contain gluten, and some varieties may include dairy or nuts. All products are prepared in the same kitchen, so traces of these ingredients may be present.

Why does your bread feel denser than packaged bread?

Our breads are made without improvers or enhancers. Whole wheat flour and natural fermentation result in a more natural structure, which can feel denser compared to commercial bread designed to be very soft and airy.

Will the texture of the bread always be the same?

Since we work with natural ingredients and small batches, slight variations in texture can occur. This is a normal part of real bread made without stabilizers or industrial controls.

How should I store the bread?

Store in a cool, dry place and avoid leaving it exposed to air, as this can cause it to dry out. For longer storage, we recommend freezing sliced bread and toasting it when needed. Refrigeration is not recommended.

Do you use preservatives or additives?

No. Our breads are made without preservatives, improvers, or commercial enhancers.

Are the images on your website real?

Yes. All images on this site are of bread we bake in our kitchen. We do not use stock or AI-generated visuals.

Can I request custom variations?

Some breads can be adjusted based on preference, such as changing the proportion of whole wheat. Availability depends on the dough and batch, so we recommend checking with us on WhatsApp before placing your order.

Do you offer 100% whole wheat bread?

100% whole wheat bread tends to be denser and less soft. We use blended flours to create a better balance of texture, flavor, and structure while still keeping a high proportion of whole wheat. If you're looking for a higher whole wheat percentage, feel free to check with us on WhatsApp.
